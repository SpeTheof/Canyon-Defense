## ğŸ’° Zone Control Bonus System (NEW!)

### Concept
Aggressive players who intercept enemies early are rewarded with bonus gold!

### How It Works
- Track each enemy's progress along the path (0% to 100%)
- If an enemy is **killed before reaching 50%** of the map:
  - **+25% Bonus Gold** awarded immediately
  - Yellow floating text "+$BONUS" appears at kill location
- **No bonus** if enemy passes 50% threshold

### Example
- Enemy reward: $20
- Zone Control Bonus: +$5 (25%)
- **Total**: $25 for early kills

### Strategy Tip
Place towers near the start of the map to maximize Zone Control bonuses!

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

1. **Upgrade strategically** - Focus upgrades on your best-positioned towers
2. **Early intercept** - Kill enemies before 50% for Zone Control bonuses
3. **Build near bends** - Towers can hit enemies longer when they're turning
4. **Get AA Guns early** - Every 5th wave is air units!
5. **Build Recyclers** - Extra income early = more towers later
6. **Use Radar towers** - The buff radius helps multiple towers
7. **Sell and reposition** - Don't be afraid to move towers as waves progress
8. **Watch the Game Log** - Top right shows real-time info
9. **Skip waves early** - 30 seconds of max bonus points!
10. **Use speed controls** - Speed up when confident, slow down for tough waves
11. **Mix tower types** - Different enemies need different solutions
